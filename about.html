About the Journal

East African Journal of Educational, Social Science and Humanities Research (ISSN 2708-8111) is a peer-reviewed, annual academic journal dedicated to the publication of original research in education, social sciences, and humanities. The journal provides a platform for scholars, researchers, and practitioners from East Africa and beyond to share findings that contribute to knowledge, policy, and practice.

Publication Frequency

The journal is published annually, with one volume per year.

Focus Areas

  • Education: Curriculum development, assessment, teaching and learning methodologies, educational policy and administration, special needs education.
  • Social Sciences: Sociology, psychology, economics, political science, anthropology, geography, development studies.
  • Humanities: Linguistics, literature, history, philosophy, cultural studies, religious studies, communication studies.

Peer Review Process

All manuscripts submitted to the journal undergo a double-blind peer review process. Each manuscript is reviewed by at least two independent reviewers with expertise in the relevant field. The review process typically takes 4–8 weeks.

Open Access

All articles published in this journal are freely available online. There are no subscription fees or paywalls. The journal is committed to making research accessible to all.

Publisher

The journal is published by the Centre for Education, Population, Environment and Development (CEPED), in collaboration with the Society of Educational Research and Evaluation in Kenya.

Indexing

The journal is indexed and archived for long-term digital preservation.
