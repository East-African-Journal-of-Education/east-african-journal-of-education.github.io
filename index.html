Aims & Scope

The East African Journal of Educational, Social Science and Humanities Research publishes original research that contributes to knowledge and policy development in East Africa and beyond. The journal welcomes manuscripts in:

  • Education — curriculum, assessment, teaching methodology, educational policy
  • Social Sciences — sociology, psychology, economics, political science, anthropology
  • Humanities — linguistics, literature, history, philosophy, cultural studies

The journal is published annually by the Centre for Education, Population, Environment and Development (CEPED).
