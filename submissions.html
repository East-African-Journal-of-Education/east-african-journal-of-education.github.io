Submission Guidelines

The East African Journal of Educational, Social Science and Humanities Research welcomes original research manuscripts from scholars and practitioners. Below are the guidelines for submission.

Manuscript Requirements

  • Language: All manuscripts must be submitted in English.
  • Length: 4,000–8,000 words including references, tables, and figures.
  • Format: Microsoft Word (.doc or .docx) or LaTeX.
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12-point, double-spaced.
  • Margins: 1 inch (2.54 cm) on all sides.
  • Referencing: APA style (7th edition) for in-text citations and reference list.

Manuscript Structure

Manuscripts should include the following sections in order:

  1. Title page — Title, author names, institutional affiliations, corresponding author email, and ORCID iD (if available).
  2. Abstract — A single paragraph of 200–300 words summarizing the purpose, methods, key findings, and conclusions. Include 4–6 keywords.
  3. Introduction — Background, problem statement, objectives, and research questions/hypotheses.
  4. Literature Review — Theoretical framework and review of relevant literature.
  5. Methodology — Research design, target population, sample, instruments, data collection, and analysis procedures.
  6. Results — Presentation of findings (tables, figures, and narrative).
  7. Discussion — Interpretation of findings in light of existing literature.
  8. Conclusion — Summary, implications, limitations, and recommendations.
  9. References — Full reference list in APA 7th edition format.
  10. Appendices (if applicable).

Submission Process

Manuscripts should be submitted via email to the Editor-in-Chief as a Word document attachment. Please include a cover letter stating that the manuscript is original, has not been published elsewhere, and is not under consideration by another journal.

Review Process

All submissions are subject to double-blind peer review. Authors will be notified of the editorial decision within 4–8 weeks of submission. The possible decisions are: acceptance, minor revision, major revision, or rejection.

Publication Fees

Authors are required to pay a publication fee upon acceptance of their manuscript. For current fee structure, please contact the editor.

Ethics

Authors must ensure that their research complies with ethical standards, including informed consent and institutional ethics approval where applicable. Plagiarism in any form is not tolerated.
